## Section 1 — What We're Building
A conversational AI music recommender that understands how you feel in plain English and finds real songs that match — not from a hardcoded list, but from a live music catalog. You talk to it like a person, it thinks, retrieves, and explains back to you.

## Section 2 — Why We're Building It
The Module 3 version proved the concept but had limits: 18 songs, rigid inputs, no real intelligence. This version removes those limits and makes the AI feel like it actually understands you — while staying honest about how it works.

## Section 3 — What Success Looks Like

Someone with no coding background can run it and get real song recommendations just by describing their mood
The AI explains why it picked each song
It asks follow-up questions to get better over time in a conversation
It handles bad inputs gracefully without crashing

## Section 4 — The AI Features We're Adding
A real music catalog pulled live from the internet (RAG)
A conversational agent that plans, retrieves, and reflects (Agentic)
Logging so we can see what the system is doing and catch mistakes
